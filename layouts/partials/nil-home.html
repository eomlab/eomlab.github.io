Pusan National University · Neural Engineering & Bioelectronics

Engineering Neural Interfaces for Personalized Brain Therapies

Our laboratory develops closed-loop, personalized brain-computer interfaces to advance the treatment and rehabilitation of neurological disorders. Using digital twin models to understand patient-specific neural characteristics, we design BCI technologies for selective, non-invasive recording and control of the three-dimensional brain and nervous system.
