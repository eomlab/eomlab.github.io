Engineering Neural Interfaces for Personalized Brain Therapies

Our laboratory develops closed-loop, personalized brain-computer interfaces for the treatment and rehabilitation of neurological disorders. Using digital twin models to capture patient-specific neural characteristics, we build BCI platforms for selective, non-invasive recording and control of the three-dimensional brain and nervous system.
