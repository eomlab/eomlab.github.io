Pusan National University · Neural Engineering & Bioelectronics

Engineering Neural Interfaces for Personalized Brain Therapies

We aim to control the three-dimensional brain and nervous system using advanced neural interfaces. By integrating brain-computer interfaces, neuromodulation, artificial intelligence, and digital twins, we develop patient-specific closed-loop strategies for neurological disorders and neural rehabilitation.
