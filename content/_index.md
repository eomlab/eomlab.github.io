      text: |-
        ### Retinal Prosthesis & Visual Neuroprosthetics
        We develop optical, electrical, and photovoltaic strategies for visual restoration, including optogenetic retinal stimulation, patterned light delivery, photovoltaic retinal implants, and in vivo validation using retinal and cortical recordings.

        ### Non-invasive Neuromodulation
        We investigate TMS, tES, and temporal interference stimulation to improve stimulation focality, reduce stimulation threshold, and develop next-generation neuromodulation systems for neurological disorders and rehabilitation.

        ### Implantable & Wireless Neural Interfaces
        We design miniaturized, battery-free, and wireless neural stimulators for chronic neuromodulation. Our work combines circuit design, electromagnetic coupling, flexible packaging, and in vivo validation.

        ### Flexible Bioelectronic Materials and Devices
        We develop flexible, stretchable, and biocompatible neural interfaces using polymeric materials, printed electronics, laser-induced graphene, and organic bioelectronic devices.

        ### Computational Neuroengineering
        We integrate electric-field modeling, cellular simulation, and whole-brain network modeling using tools such as SIMNIBS, NEURON, NetPyNE, and The Virtual Brain.
